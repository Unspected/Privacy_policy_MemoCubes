Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled by the mobile application "Memo Cubes" ("we," "our," or "us"). Our primary goal is to create a safe and enjoyable memory game for everyone, especially children.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We are proud to be a minimal-data game. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal Information: We do not ask for or collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or physical addresses.
  • Usage Data: We do not use third-party analytics services to track how you play the game. We do not know which levels you play, your scores, or your in-game behavior.
  • Device Information: We do not collect unique device identifiers, IP addresses, or information about your device type or operating system.
  • Location Data: The game does not collect, use, or share your geographic location.
  • Children's Data: We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

2. Information We May Collect (Non-Personal)

The only data the game stores is locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and we cannot access it.

  • Game Progress: Your current level, high score, and game settings are stored locally on your device to allow you to continue your game. This data is stored only in your device's local memory and can be erased by uninstalling the app.

3. Third-Party Services

Memo Cubes is a standalone game. It does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect data. Specifically:

  • No Advertising: The game does not display any advertisements from ad networks.
  • No Social Media: The game does not connect to any social media platforms.
  • No In-App Purchases: The game does not offer any in-app purchases.

4. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal or usage data, there is no information for us to use.

The local game progress data is used solely to provide the game's core functionality and remember your place.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not collect any personal data. Therefore, we have nothing to share, sell, or disclose to any third parties.

6. Parental Rights and Controls (COPPA Compliance)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Since we do not collect any personal information from children, parental control over such information is not needed.

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with any personal information (which our app is not designed to do), please contact us immediately, and we will work with you to address your concerns.

7. Your Consent

By downloading and playing Memo Cubes, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
